Explore Studio Elttob.

  • Elttob Suite

    Collection of professionally-produced plugins for Roblox Studio, with longstanding favourites like Elttob Reclass and new ideas like Elttob Relight.

  • Fusion

    The most popular granular reactive library in Luau, built to be stable and predictable at scale. It powers all Studio Elttob products under the hood.

  • Vanilla

    Icon set built to be legible while maintaining a distinct visual identity. It's the icon style used throughout all Studio Elttob products.

  • Volumika

    Finely-tuned and performant volumetric lighting package. With unbeaten compatibility and carefully calibrated colour, it's been used in some of Roblox's largest productions yet.

Hi. I'm Daniel P H Fox.

I'm a longtime Roblox community member and creator advocate. Studio Elttob is my personal brand for the products I independently create and sell on Roblox.

I want to make tools that you can feel good about using, and set a positive example for how to run a sustainable, organic, creator-focused business.

New tools with new perspectives.

The goal of Studio Elttob is to explore experimental directions and pursue new paradigms to enable uniquely powerful modes of creations.

At the heart of the design process, I analyse each part of the creation workflow. I look to tackle existing problems in new ways, searching for distinctive or overlooked design ideas.

Designed with care and precision.

Every moment you use a Studio Elttob product, you should feel like a human spent a lot of time to get every detail right. I strive to present my craft with pride.

That's why I focus a lot of effort on design. Everything in the portfolio isn't just functional, but beautiful. When it just works and just clicks, it's just right.

Built to give you dignity.

Products these days are tiring. From exaggerated marketing to broken trust, there's a lot of turbulence and lost hope. I don't want Studio Elttob to be a part of this.

I want to build a good-old set of things that are truly your own, respecting your intelligence and your time. You should feel good about software again; it shouldn't be a game of chess.
