Explore Studio Elttob.

  • Elttob Suite

    Collection of professionally-produced plugins for Roblox Studio, with longstanding favourites like Elttob Reclass and new ideas like Elttob Relight.

  • Fusion

    The most popular granular reactive library in Luau, built to be stable and predictable at scale. It powers all Studio Elttob products under the hood.

  • Vanilla

    Icon set built to be legible while maintaining a distinct visual identity. It's the icon style used throughout all Studio Elttob products.

  • Volumika

    Finely-tuned and performant volumetric lighting package. With unbeaten compatibility and carefully calibrated colour, it's been used in some of Roblox's largest projects.

Hi. I'm Daniel P H Fox.

I'm a longtime Roblox community member and creator advocate. Studio Elttob is my personal brand for the products I independently create and sell in my free time.

I want to make tools that are practically useful for creators, and I want to build a business that's sustainable and simple.

New tools with new perspectives.

The goal of Studio Elttob is to explore experimental directions and pursue new paradigms to enable uniquely powerful modes of creations.

At the heart of the design process, I analyse each part of the creation workflow. I look for under-represented problems that could be tackled in new ways.

Designed with care and precision.

I strive to present my craft with pride. Every moment you use a Studio Elttob product, you should feel like a human spent a lot of time to get every detail right.

That's why I focus a lot of effort on design. Everything in the portfolio isn't just functional, but beautiful. When it just works and just clicks, it's just right.

Simple and sensible.

I'm not in the business business, so to speak. I want Studio Elttob products to present clear value without overhyped marketing or shady optimisation.

So, I keep it simple. You can just buy what's in the box. Then, it's yours to use, and you can focus on what you want to do next. I strive to make Studio Elttob a sustainable, creator-first role model for others to be inspired by.
